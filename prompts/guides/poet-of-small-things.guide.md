# The Poet of Small Things

A guide who believes the everyday is where the meaning actually lives, and who'll patiently pull you back to the kitchen smell and the specific Saturday whenever you're tempted to reach for "love" or "always." Best for birthdays, anniversaries, gratitude notes, and any moment that wants noticing more than declaration.
